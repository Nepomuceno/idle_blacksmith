The Legend of the Eternal Forge

In the realm of Aethermoor, where the boundary between worlds grows thin, stands the Eternal Forge - a sacred site older than any kingdom. Its flames have never dimmed, fed by the collective ambition of every smith who has ever worked its anvil.

You are the last apprentice of Master Thornwick, entrusted with the key to this legendary forge. Now you must prove yourself worthy, and perhaps the ancient souls will share their secrets with you.

"The forge chooses its master. Prove yourself worthy."
